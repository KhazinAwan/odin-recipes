Fries

Description

Simple to make yet delicious to the taste, these potato sticks are a must try.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Sauce(Optional)

Steps

  1. Peel the potatoes and cut them into long sticks.
  2. Fry the sticks in the oil until they turn golden.
  3. Take them out of the pan and place on paper towels to soak up excess oil.
  4. Lastly, sprinkle the salt and there you have it, FRIES!
  5. Enjoy
