Descrition

Nothing is more mouth-watering than this Italian tower of dripping cheese together with the divine meat sauce!

Ingredients

  1. Ricotta cheese
  2. Parmesan cheese
  3. Mozarella chees
  4. Onion
  5. Garlic
  6. Olive oil
  7. Lasagna noodels
  8. Tomato sauce
  9. Ground beef
  10. Salt
  11. Pepper
  12. Italian herbs

Steps

  1. Firstly, you need a preheated oven.
  2. Start by cooking your Lasagna noodles.
  3. Add chopped onion and garlic to a pan with olive oil.
  4. Next add the beef and the tomato sauce to make your Lasagna meat sauce.You can also add the Italian herbs.
  5. Take a baking dish , and a layer of the Lasagna noodles you prepared earlier, then some meat sauce and finally add cheese.
  6. Repeat layering in this manner until the prepared ingeredients are used up.
  7. Top it with some cheese and then leave it in the preheated oven from 40 mins.
  8. Wait for the cheese to turn golden and there oyu have it, THE LASAGNA!
  9. Enjoy.
